About Me

Dr. Tariq Aziz is a leading Pakistani soil scientist specializing in sustainable nutrient management, with particular focus on nitrogen and phosphorus use in agriculture. He serves as Associate Professor at the Institute of Soil & Environmental Sciences, University of Agriculture Faisalabad.

As Regional Director (South Asia) of the International Nitrogen Initiative (INI), he represents South Asia in global efforts to balance nitrogen use for food security and environmental protection. His research bridges the gap between agricultural productivity and environmental sustainability.

He has supervised numerous Ph.D. and M.Sc. students and maintains active collaborations with international universities including the University of Giessen (Germany) and University of Western Australia.
